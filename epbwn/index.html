About Us

Expat Professional and Business Women’s Network Consultants International(EPBWN) is a diverse group of professional consultants who specialize in fashioning and enhancing the advancement of women in the workplace, SME’s , private firms, and international institutions. Through our specialize trainings, collaborations and business partnerships we have been able to create opportunities for coaching, mentorships and work placement programmes across continents. We also provide pioneering opportunities for women to use their strengths, talents and skills in supporting each other for financial freedom and expansion of their businesses. EPBWN Consultants International has been in existence since 2013

Currently, our professional consultants are scattered in different countries in different continents, they are in Tunisia, Kenya, Malawi, South Africa, Ethiopia, Nigeria, South Korea, Singapore, Philippines, Cote’D’Ivoire, Belgium, Lesotho, Uganda, Tanzania ,Zambia, Zimbabwe, Gambia, Ghana, USA, Britain, Brazil, Netherlands and India By creating and providing sustainable engaging communities, we have been able to provide an avenue to offer support, personal and professional development for women and our clients across the globe.

We achieve this through networking, advocacy and training activities that build human capacity. We have successful collaborations and partnership with African Development Bank (AfDB), various Embassies , International Organisations, Individuals, NGOS, Korean National Sport University and University of Jos. We believe that behind every successful woman there is a tribe of other successful women who have her back. It does not take much for one candle to light another resulting in a beautiful and glowing flame therefore we should never underestimate the power of women with a collective goal of sustainability for women worldwide.

Our Objectives

  • To bring together a team of professionals and business owners to promote development of their careers resulting in financial stability.
  • To provide a platform for international businesses to promote trade and commerce across the continents.
  • To assist women with educational, health and financial training programmes geared towards advancing their personal growth and career development.
  • To create a forum for gender equality, networking, collaborations between individuals, financial institutions, diplomatic communities and international organisations.
  • To establish and facilitate events, activities that will advance the interest and growth of our clients.
  • To advocate for the welfare of women in the workspace both locally and internationally through promoting visibility.

Expat Professional and Business Women’s Consultants International has provided stellar gender development services to organizations around the globe over the past decade.

OUR VISION

  • To be one of the top-ranking consulting firms that specializes in human development, business creation and financial funding
  • To revolutionize gender equality across workspaces globally for the next generation.
  • To promote social justice, diversity, equality, inclusion and dynamism in the work environment that is innovational with the changing societal values.
  • To providing coaching and mentorship opportunities for young professionals that will enable them aspire beyond their regions.
  • To provide a personalized, proficient and affordable client support package both locally and internationally.
  • To set the stage for international networking, collaborations and business partnerships.

OUR MISSON

  • To provide an exceptional professional human and business development services that creates financial empowerment, promote personal and career development.
  • To encourage affirmative action that gears towards gender equality that builds, stabilizes and improves the lives of women in the workplace.
  • To revamp and rebrand organizational reputation to create a new and acceptable image of our clients.
  • To encourage and enhance change in governance structure of our clients to enable growth and well being in workspaces.
  • To develop specialized leadership retreats, seminars, summits, workshops and training modules that will expand capacity.
